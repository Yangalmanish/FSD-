About Us

The primary motive of our application is to eliminate the traditional buyer and sell further up the food chain is to save money. Manufacturers today sell directly to consumers thanks to Internet expansion. Rather than paying sales representatives to promote products to resellers, companies can promote products on their own websites, take orders and send goods directly to the final customer. Eliminating the middleman usually creates a win-win for the seller and buyer from a money perspective. Each step in a traditional distribution process involves a trade buyer adding a mark-up to his costs. This ultimately makes the final customer's price higher because he is paying for the original product costs, the costs of each buyer's acquisition as well as the profit expected by the retailer. By getting rid of the middleman's mark-ups, you can offer the customer a lower price while getting higher gross profits for Farmers. This application will help farmer to check the rates of the cultivation directly in reference with the Big Companies and compare the rate between them before dealing. After deal is completed between Farmer and The Company the shipment is delivered .This Application will eliminate the Middle man and both farmer as well as Company will have Successful deal.
